Primed (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides tools that help businesses manage Instagram and Facebook direct messages (DMs) with AI assistance. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Primed or connect your Meta accounts.

Information We Receive

  • DM content & metadata: message text, sender/recipient IDs, timestamps, conversation IDs, and media links/IDs from connected Instagram/Facebook assets.
  • Account details: page/account IDs, workspace/admin name and email, permissions granted.
  • Operational data: API/webhook events, logs, diagnostics, IP/device info of admins.
  • Optional lead fields: name, email, phone, booking times, tags/notes you choose to store.
Sensitive data: Do not use Primed to collect health, financial, or other sensitive categories. If such content is detected, we aim to avoid storage and may guide users not to share it.

How We Use Information

  • Provide the service: ingest/display DMs, generate AI-assisted replies, qualify leads, and book appointments.
  • Maintain and improve reliability, security, and performance (e.g., debugging, abuse prevention).
  • Comply with platform and legal obligations and honor user requests.

Sharing

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) as needed for API functionality and compliance.
  • Service providers (hosting, AI inference, analytics, monitoring) under confidentiality and data processing agreements.
  • Regulators/authorities if required by law.

We do not sell or rent personal information.

Data Retention & Deletion

Message content is typically retained for up to 30 days to support active conversations and audit, then deleted or anonymized. Logs may be kept longer for security/compliance. You can request deletion at any time; we will complete deletion from active systems within 30 days, subject to legal retention.

AI Use & Human Oversight

  • Primed may send message text to AI models to draft replies or assess intent.
  • Admins control tone/guardrails and can review or override outputs.

Security

  • HTTPS/TLS in transit; encryption at rest where supported.
  • Role-based access, least-privilege keys, and audit logging.
  • Monitoring for abuse and unauthorized access.
